How to read it

The colored field is an inverse-distance interpolation between monitoring stations (dots), so it estimates AQI between sensors the way a weather radar fills gaps between returns.

Press play (or hit space) to sweep through the 48-hour window, or drag the scrubber. Tap a station to pin its reading in the readout.

Until you connect live data, values are simulated — the timestamps are real but the plume is fiction.
