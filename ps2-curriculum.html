PlayStation 2 — Gaming Curriculum

Am I Still a Gamer?

Two parallel tracks. Play alongside GTA: San Andreas. Track B for quick sessions, Track A when you're ready to sink in. Expand any game and tap "What Critics Said" for the review consensus and links.

How to Use This

GTA: San Andreas is your ongoing main game. Always have it going.

Track B games are your palate cleansers — play these in shorter sessions, especially when you want something SFW or low-commitment.

Track A games are your main courses after San Andreas. Play them roughly in order — each one builds on the vocabulary of the last.

If a game doesn't click after its evaluation window, move on guilt-free. Not every classic is for everyone, and that's real talk.
