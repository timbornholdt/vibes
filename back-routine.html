How to use this routine

Daily, ~10–12 min.

Workout days: do the dynamic moves as a pre-workout warm-up, and the holds in the evening as a wind-down.

Rest days: do the whole routine in the evening in order.

Expect noticeable improvement within 2–3 weeks of daily consistency. Some baseline morning stiffness is normal — discs rehydrate overnight; the goal is to move it out, not erase it.
